Eurocoat 30-36

EUROCOAT 30-36 Is a water based protective coating for thermal insulation application and excellent adhesion to canvas and other insulation materials. It is a white, fire resistive, tough, washable coating. It can use as a lagging and lap adhesive for canvas and glass lagging cloth. it has excellent brushing characteristics, whch will result in better coverage. INOCOAT 30-36 provides a protective finish for insulation on air conditioning ducts and cold water piping, when applied in two coats with reinforcing fabric embedded between coats.

Instruction for use

All surface to be used, must be sound and throughly clean befor INOFIX 701 is applied.
Mix with water throughly until asmoth uniform paste is formed.
A slow speed drill fited with a mixing paddle is recommended, The preparation past should be spread on the substrate to a uniform thickness of 2-3mm and then combed horizontally.
Place tiles firmly in to adhesivebed, ensureing good contact with a twisting motion.

Coverage : 3-5 kg/m² depending upon the thickness of bed and surface to be applied.

Storage : Should be stored in warehouse condition, keep away from moisture and humidity.

Shelf Life : 12 month from the date of manufacture in original packing, if stored properly.

Packing : 20 kg Bags.

Color : White / Grey.

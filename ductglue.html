Duct Glue 81-10

TECHNO DUCT 81 - 10 is a high quality adhesive designed to bond all types of fibrous and non- fibrous insulation materials to G.I. aluminum, wood steel, concrete, and wide variety of materials.
USES:TECHNO DUCT 81-10 is used for bonding different types of insulating materials to themselves and to all metals such as Galvanized Iron, aluminum air conditioning ducts and hot ducts. It,s also used for insulation materials such as rock wool, polyurethane, and phenolic foam.
Note: TECHNO DUCT 81-10 is not suitable for use with expanded polystyrene, polyethylene, or soft PVC.
ADVANTAGES:Durable,Excellent permanent,Resistance to water, dilute acid, and alkalis suitable for bonding wide range of insulation materials,Easy to apply,Economical.

Instruction for use

Surfaces must be cleaned, dried, and free from dust, grease, and loose materials. Use a good brush making strokes as long as possible over the surface. Apply with full brush and spread evenly.Wash tools with water before product dries.

Coverage : 6-8 m²/Lit depends on surface condition.

Storage : Should be stored in warehouse condition, keep away from moisture and humidity.

Shelf Life : 12 month from the date of manufacture in original packing, if stored properly.

Packing : 18 kg Pail.

Color : Light Green / Red.

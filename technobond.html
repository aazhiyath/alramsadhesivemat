U.B. TECHNOBOND -50

TECHNO BOND-50 is a resin based multipurpose adhesive for the construction industry as a bonding additive to concrete and plasters. Upon drying it forms a strong film which is non-staining and non -discoloring.
USES: For concrete repairing,For building up of compression, To give greater adhesion to mortar and plaster. As a general purpose adhesive for bonding-Tiles, Concrete, Roofing, Linoleum polystyrene etc. As a primer for over coating bitumen
ADVANTAGES: Alkali resistant, Can be applied to damp surface, without puddles or brittleness. Once cured, it is flexible and remains unaffected to climatic and weather conditions. Works on traction and shearing. Non hazardous & non toxic. Can be used as a filler by diluting 1: 1 with water, and add suitable bulk ingredient such as plaster, clean fine sand

Storage : Should be stored in warehouse condition, keep away from moisture and humidity.

Shelf Life : 12 month from the date of manufacture in original packing, if stored properly.

Packing : TECHNO BOND-50 is available in 5 liter Can, 20 kg Can and 200 kg Drum.

Color : White Milky

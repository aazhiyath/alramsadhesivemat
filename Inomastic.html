INOROCK(Gypsum joint compound)

INOROCK Is ready to use acrylic filler used for filling joints on all common building surfaces. This high solid smooth paste specially used for joint filling of gypsum boards and as fine coat filler for patching of crack on wall etc. It provides a high quality and smooth surface for painting. INOROCK acrylic filler is suitable for exterior as well as interior surfaces.

Instruction for use

All surfaces to be applied must be free from dust, dirt, oil, grease or any loose particle. Fix self adhesive fiberglass tape on to the joints of gypsum board. Apply INOROCK filler in a thin layer. If the area under repair require a thick coat, apply several thin layers. Allow each layer to dry before applying next coat. Saturate the surface with primer is recommended before the application within 24 hours of application before painting.

Storage : Should be stored in warehouse condition, keep away from moisture and humidity.

Shelf Life : 12 month from the date of manufacture in original packing, if stored properly.

Packing : 20 kg / 5 kg Pail.

Color : White

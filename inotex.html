INOTEX(Crack Filler)

INOFILLER is a cement based polymer modified powder material for filling cracks in plastered surface. It is composed of cement, polymer, selected aggregates and additives it has strong adhesion good water resistance properties and is non-shrink in nature. It can be used to fill static cracks of 3 to 5mm, on horizontal, vertical and over head surface. It adheres to common building substrates. It enhances the subsequent painting quality and life.

Instruction for use

All surfaces to be applied should be sound and thoroughly clean. Remove all dust, dirt, rust, oil, grease and any loose particle, which will affect the adhesion of the filler with the substrate. Make sure that surface is clean and free from standing water. Normally INOFILLER does not require a primer however in critical areas acrylic based primer is recommended. INOFILLER should be mixed thoroughly with water to get a thicker pasty consistency. Fill the mixed paste in to the crack by spatula or putty knife, pressing against crack so that all voids get filled.

Storage : Should be stored in warehouse condition, keep away from moisture and humidity.

Shelf Life : 12 month from the date of manufacture in original packing, if stored properly.

Packing : 30 kg Pail.
